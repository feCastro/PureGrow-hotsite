Testing, Cleaning & Disinfection

Our process starts with testing the local water and air quality of your space. Then the cleaning process begins using a powerful Bioproduct designed for deep cleaning, followed by an antimicrobial liquid to disinfect all surfaces, creating a pathogen- free environment prior to the installation of our PureGrow Coatings.

Electrostatic Spraying

Electrostatic Sprayers achieve greater spray coverage by combining air turbulence with tiny, evenly-sized spray droplets which produces an even coating with the antimicrobial liquid that achieves a much better disease controlled environment. When our products are used as directed and applied with electrostatic spraying technology, they have proven to dramatically reduce, and in most cases, eliminate the presence of mold, mildew, fungus, and other pathogens including Aspergillus, within the total environment.

Coatings

Advanced Surface Protection for Optimal Plant Growth.

Our cutting-edge coatings harness advanced biotechnology to protect your cultivation environment. Activated by sunlight or ambient conditions, these coatings shield surfaces from mold, mildew, fungi, bacteria, and viruses, including the notorious Aspergillus.

In the presence of light, the TiO2 component initiates a photocatalytic oxidation (PCO) process. When exposed to light sources—such as UV or halogen lights—the TiO2-coated surface releases hydroxyl radicals (OH-) and superoxide anions (O2-), highly reactive agents that destroy harmful pathogens by breaking down their cellular structure.

These radicals quickly oxidize contaminants that come into contact with the coated surface, converting them into harmless by-products like CO2 and H2O. This process not only purifies the air but also reduces odors and volatile organic compounds (VOCs), improving overall air quality.

NanoBubbles (Generator)

Our NanoBubbles technology infuses water with high levels of O2, enhancing nutrient absorption at the root level. This leads to stronger, disease-resistant plants with improved growth and yield.

This technology is designed to help keep irrigation lines clean and to provide the ability to uptake nutrients to the root level of the plants.

This results in larger plants increasing buds and trichomes while also helping ward off pathogens through the root system. NanoBubbles are infused into the water producing high levels of oxygen being fed to the root system which helps with the nutrient uptake process, providing a stronger and disease resistant plant.

NanoSilver®

Continuous Surface and Air Purification, Day and Night.

NanoSilver® is a revolutionary hybrid coating combining titanium dioxide (TiO2) and nanosilver, offering powerful, round-the-clock protection against bacteria, viruses, mold, mildew, and fungi. Designed to work in both light and dark environments, NanoSilver® creates an ideal space for health and growth.

With its unique combination of TiO2 and NanoSilver® delivers unparalleled protection in all lighting conditions—whether through the photocatalytic action of TiO2 in the light or the antibacterial, antiviral properties of NanoSilver® in the dark.

This constant regeneration of reactive particles ensures long-lasting cleanliness and safety, helping to create pathogen-free environments for up to three years.
