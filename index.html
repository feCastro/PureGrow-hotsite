Main Benefits*

Testing, Cleaning & Disinfection

Our process starts with Biotex, a powerful bio-product designed for deep cleaning, followed by Penetrexx Antimicrobial to disinfect every surface, light, and pipe, ensuring a pathogen-free environment.

Electrostatic Spraying

We apply Purelight®, a coating activated by ambient light, and NanoSilver®, which provides continuous protection even in the absence of light. This dual-layer defense drastically reduces mold, mildew, fungi, bacteria, and viruses.

Nanobubbles (Generator)

Our NanoBubble technology infuses water with high levels of O2, enhancing nutrient absorption at the root level. This leads to stronger, disease-resistant plants with improved growth and yield.

Photocatalytic Oxidation

Photocatalytic oxidation (PCO) is the process of converting malignant contaminants into water, carbon dioxide and detritus. The photocatalytic method is ideal for water treatment and air purification. The technology works by breaking down the pollutants to improve the quality of air and water.
