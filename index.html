NanoSilver®

Continuous Surface and Air Purification, Day and Night.

NanoSilver® is a revolutionary hybrid coating combining titanium dioxide (TiO2) and nanosilver, offering powerful, round-the-clock protection against bacteria, viruses, mold, mildew, and fungi. Designed to work in both light and dark environments, NanoSilver® creates an ideal space for health and growth.

In the presence of light, the TiO2 component initiates a photocatalytic oxidation (PCO) process. When exposed to light sources—such as UV or halogen lights—the TiO2-coated surface releases hydroxyl radicals (OH-) and superoxide anions (O2-), highly reactive agents that destroy harmful pathogens by breaking down their cellular structure. These radicals quickly oxidize contaminants that come into contact with the coated surface, converting them into harmless by-products like CO2 and H2O. This process not only purifies the air but also reduces odors and volatile organic compounds (VOCs), improving overall air quality.

However, what sets NanoSilver® apart is its dual-action capability. In spaces where light is unavailable or insufficient, the nanosilver component takes over, ensuring 24/7 protection. Silver ions (Ag+) are released from the nanosilver particles and effectively attack bacteria, viruses, and fungi. The silver ions disrupt cell walls, damage DNA and RNA, and prevent the microorganisms from replicating. This process continues even in complete darkness, making NanoSilver® effective in any condition.

For optimal results, NanoSilver® coatings should be applied over large surface areas, as a greater coated area increases the reactive surface, speeding up the purification process. Additionally, the coating ensures that radicals generated during the PCO process remain confined to the surface, making it safe for plants, animals, and humans. The only by-products released are CO2 and H2O, which are beneficial for plants, promoting a healthier growing environment.
